1. Orders Dashboard – Real-Time Sales Visibility

This dashboard gives Sales Directors an instant view of commercial performance with:

Key Features

£43M+ total sales tracking with drilldowns

Total orders, total customers, and monthly breakdowns

Sales by Sales Area & Sales Manager

Interactive filtering by:

Sales Area

Sales Manager

Year

Month

Sales Director

Product

Top 10 Customers and Top 10 Products

Revenue trends with forecasting

Sales contribution across territories

Drillthrough to customer-level records

Impact

Replaced multiple manual Excel reports

Gave leadership the ability to track revenue daily

Improved transparency across all regions and sales teams

Helped identify high-performing products & customer segments

⭐ 2. Conversion Dashboard – Quote to Sale Performance

This dashboard analyses how effectively quotes convert into sales across the organisation.

Key Features

Full conversion rate calculation across all Sales Areas

Converted vs. Not Converted value by month

Revenue leakage analysis (Unconverted Quotation Value): £94M+

Sales Order value recovered from converted quotes

Conversion by:

Sales Area

Sales Director

Month

Status (Converted / Direct / Not Converted)

Detailed breakdown table with quotes, sales, month, value, and conversion status

Impact

Identified Sales Areas with the strongest & weakest conversion

Highlighted where revenue was being lost

Enabled targeted coaching and process improvement

Provided actionable insight for pipeline prioritisation

⭐ 3. Metrics Dashboard – Executive KPI Overview

A high-level metrics dashboard designed for leadership meetings, covering:

Key Features

MTD, QTD, and YTD sales displayed cleanly & instantly

Top 10 Product performance treemap

Detailed mapping of customers across the UK

Regional performance (North, South, Private OT/Case Mgmt, etc.)

Year over Year comparison via slicers

Dynamic filtering by month, year, sales area, and director

Impact

Provided an easy-to-read, board-ready KPI view

Helped regions benchmark performance

Highlighted product trends and geographical hot-spots

Became the primary tool for monthly sales reviews
